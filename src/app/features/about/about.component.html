Meet Nick Reinoso

Hi! I’m Nick Reinoso, the baker behind The Caffeinated Baker. Born and raised in Texas, I grew up surrounded by delicious food and family traditions. My love for cooking started early, watching FoodTV as a kid and experimenting with dessert recipes.

My mom was my first cooking teacher, sharing her family recipes and teaching me the basics. With her guidance and plenty of practice, I became hooked on creating sweet treats—probably because of my serious sweet tooth!

Throughout high school and college, I continued to explore the world of baking and cooking, eventually turning it into a side gig. From selling cakes to friends and family to making a wedding cake for my best friend, baking has become more than just a hobby for me.

The Caffeinated Baker is my way of sharing the recipes and techniques that have brought joy and delicious moments to my loved ones. Whether it’s a batch of brownies or a hearty meal, I hope my blog will inspire you to create your own flavorful memories.
