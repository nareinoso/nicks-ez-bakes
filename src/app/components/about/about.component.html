Meet Nick Reinoso

Hi! I’m Nick Reinoso, the baker behind The Caffeinated Baker. Born and raised in Texas, I grew up surrounded by delicious food and family traditions. My love for cooking started early, watching FoodTV as a kid and experimenting with dessert recipes. My mom, Priscilla, was my first cooking teacher, sharing her family recipes and teaching me the basics. With her guidance and plenty of practice, I became hooked on creating sweet treats—probably because of my serious sweet tooth!

Throughout high school and college, I continued to explore the world of baking and cooking, eventually turning it into a side gig. From selling cakes to friends and family to making a wedding cake for my best friend, baking has become more than just a hobby for me. My fondness for kitchen gadgets and techniques has only grown over the years, and I’m always looking for new ways to improve my skills.

The Caffeinated Baker is my way of sharing the recipes and techniques that have brought joy and delicious moments to my loved ones. Whether it’s a batch of brownies or a hearty meal, I hope my blog will inspire you to create your own flavorful memories.

Thanks for stopping by and happy baking!
